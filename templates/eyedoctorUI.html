ABOUT EYE DOCTOR

Eyes are our window to the world around us. Hence taking good care of them is of utmost importance. Owing to our busy lifestyle, we tend to give less importance to eye care and end up delaying checkups with the doctor until its too late. Progression of these eye diseases can only be prevented if they are recognized accurately at an early stage. Our idea is based on the saying “Prevention is better than cure”, where we would like to provide an easy approach for automated eye disease recognition using digital image processing and machine learning techniques such as deep convolution neural network (DCNN). The unique feature here is the fact that this eye checkup facility can be used by anyone with a Dell laptop/computer as a desktop application.
